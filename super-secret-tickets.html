The Internet
Christmas Party

Thursday 18th of December, 7pm till close
Downstairs @ The Shoreditch
Tickets: £5 min donation
(all proceeds go to Shelter)

The Internet Christmas Party is for internet working freelancers, small company folk and anyone else who needs some holiday cheer. Our lovely sponsors are providing some drinks on the evening, and we’ll be providing the good cheer. We'll have some extra special surprises on the night, so keep an eye on the hashtag on Twitter for some sneak peeks... Come along with your cheesy jumpers, jolly Christmas singing voices and penchant for mulled beverages.

If you want to read about the birth of The Internet Christmas Party, then you can read Luke’s diatribe on Medium.

The Shoreditch in...
um...
Shoreditch

We've got the entire downstairs of The Shoreditch booked out for our Christmas partyers. There's a side for dancing, music and festivities, and a side for quieter chat so we make sure that everyone is catered for. You'll need to bring some ID so we can check you off the list.

The closest tubes are Shoreditch High St (5 minute walk), Old St (9 minute walk), Liverpool St (11 minute walk). There are a multitude of buses from Central London (including the 243 and 55) that stop just up the road as well.

The folks making this possible

It's said to the point of cliché, but we wanted to stress that this party wouldn't be happening without the support of these guys. They have made the venue, the bar and all the fun things we've got planned possible with their support, so show them some love back.
