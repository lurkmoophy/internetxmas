The Internet
Christmas Party

A christmas party in London for internet-working Freelancers, small folks & others who need some holiday love.

'Tis The Season

Firstly, thank you very much for your interest in The Internet Christmas Party! If you're a freelancer, a small agency or someone who just doesn't get a party, this is for you. We're going to get together for one evening for a cheesy christmas jamboree; with drinks, badly knitted jumpers and a few other things that wouldn't look out of place in your obligatory (but more enjoyable!) office or family do.

Grab A Ticket!

Due to your keen interest, we're releasing tickets to everyone who signed up first. In order to come to the party, you will need to buy a ticket. All ticket proceeds will be donated to the Shelter charity, which campaigns to end homelessness in England and Scotland. We're asking that everyone buy a £5 ticket. Some of you expressed an interest in donating more, so we have higher cost tickets for that purpose. As long as you buy a £5 ticket then you will be able to enter this christmas extravaganza. Get your tickets below!

When and where

On the 18th of December we'd like you to make your way to the 'The Shoreditch' bar at 145 Shoreditch High Street, London, E1 6JE. The festivities start at 7PM and we'll be going until around 11PM. Although not required, you may want to wear a Christmasy Jumper for the maximum experience.
